Gap Filling in Images - Modified Direct Sampling Approach / Python Code

Project Description

The present study is part of my M.Sc. thesis.
In recent years, an increasing amount of data is being generated from all over the world. This abundance in data resources (e.g., satellite imagery, field measurements, etc.) has helped push scientific research forward and allowed experts to better understand and prepare for the environmental challenges that affect our countries. However, one critical issue that keeps limiting most experts is in regards to the presence of gaps in our datasets. This recurring problem, if not handled approprietly, can in many cases lead to misleading and biased results. Thus, the use of gap filling methods is of paramount importance. The Direct Sampling method is one such method that was implemented by different researchers all over the world to fill gaps in satellite imagery, soil maps, etc. The present work proposes two modifications to this method that were proposed to improve its overall performance.
